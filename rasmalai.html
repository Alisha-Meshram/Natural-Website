Rasmalai Tiramisu

Home - Malai - Rasmalai Tiramisu

Ingredients:

- 7-8 Lady finger biscuits
- 5-6 scoops Malai ice cream
- 10-12 strands Kashmiri saffron
- ¼ cup Mascarpone cheese
- 5pcs Rasmalai
- 2tbsp Chopped Pistachios
- 1 tsp dried rose petals

Method:

1. Mix two scoops of malai ice cream with the saffron.

2. Dip the lady finger biscuits in this mixture.

3. Whip up the mascarpone cheese with 3 scoops of malai ice cream until smooth.

4. Chop the Rasmalai into smaller pieces.

5. Layer them alternately.

6. Top it up with some more Rasmalai, dried rose petals and chopped pistachios

7. Serve chilled & enjoy!
